Weather and the seasons

Weather is one of those inexplicable elements of life that effects every human on Earth. It has the ability to change moods, and has been the reason for countless ancient religions.

The Seasons

Spring is the season of rebirth: it is associated with positive change. Cherry blossoms, as shown above, are commonly used to represent this season.
Fall is a season epitomized by change. The leaves traverse through all colors of the rainbow; the weather becomes windy and erratic.
A classic winter is white and bitter -- but the holidays early on tend to offset the season's inherent bleakness
Most people's favorite season, summer is characterized by freedom and relaxation. There's a reason time seems to move quicker during these months.

Everyone complains about the weather, but nobody ever seems to do anything about it.

-Willard Scott
